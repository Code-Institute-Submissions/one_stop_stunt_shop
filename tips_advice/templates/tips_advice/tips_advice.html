Any Motorbike Can Stunt!

Kawasaki Ninja 250/300

Level

The Kawasaki Ninja 250/300 is a great motorbike to learn wheelies, or to begin stunting with. The light weight and easy power will help speed up the learning process. We highly recommend this motorbike for someone who is looking to learn stunting but is worried about the weight and power of a 600cc.

Honda CB/CBR 650F

Level

The Honda CB/CBR650f Isn’t the first bike that comes to mind when you think about stunt riding. However we have built one and tested it to its limits and can gladly say it is a very compatible stunt bike (once the correct stunt parts are added). The naked bike style of the bike is very beneficial as there are no faring to get in the way of your riding. There isn’t a large market for stunt parts, But we can get it all for you!

BMW S1000 RR

Level

The BMW S1000 RR Isn’t the first bike that comes to mind when you think about stunt riding. However this german monster is the king of bikes to master for stunt riding. The masive amount of power makes it hard to control. Most riders who ride this bike for stunting do an engine remap to lower the power and make it more managable

Kawasaki z800

Level

The Kawasaki Z800 is a challanging bike to begin stunting with. This is due to the heavy weight of the bike along with the engine it make the process of learning wheelies much harder then on other bikes. Secondly the frame of this bike is not suited for stunt riding. The mounting points are weak and the streights in terms of stunting are not strong enough.
